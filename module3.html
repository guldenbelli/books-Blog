Reflective Pages: Module 3

Third module: Skills of the IS Professional

Module Tutor: Fehmida Mohamedali and Liz Sokolovzki

This third and final module for the first Semester was enjoyable, not too hard (personally). We worked in groups as a first part of the module, learning how to make a good and efficient Power Point Presentation, with a given theme. My group did a good job.
As the second part of the module, we have been practicing skills on Excel from the Microsoft Office package. What we learned was really useful and I am greatful to my Tutors for being able to explain the subject easily and for being there to explain further.
